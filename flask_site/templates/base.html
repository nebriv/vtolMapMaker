Here's a bad frontend for my VTOL VR Map Generator. No promises.

Some Notes...

  • Roads are really janky, you may not be able to interact with them in VTOL.
  • Airports are really janky, you may not be able to interact with them in VTOL.
  • The city paint is configurable using the "City Offset" slider. This changes how much city paint will be applied.
  • Yes, I know not all edge types are supported.
  • I believe the resolution should be fairly close to appropriate scale in vtol. I've tried to match what you'll see in the game based on the zoom levels in map below. Its not perfect, but should work.
  • The heights should be close to scale, you can adjust them further with the offset slider.
